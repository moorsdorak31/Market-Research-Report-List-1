<p><p>Barrier packaging involves materials designed to protect contents from external factors like moisture, oxygen, and light. Key types include PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), known for its strength and clarity; CPP (Cast Polypropylene), which offers excellent moisture barrier properties; and BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene), valued for its transparency and low seal initiation temperature. PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) is biodegradable and water-soluble, suitable for eco-friendly applications. PLA (Polylactic Acid) is a bioplastic derived from renewable resources, promoting sustainability. Other materials may include various composites and advanced polymers.</p></p>
