<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave</li><li>Mid-wave</li><li>Long-wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal imaging cameras are categorized by their spectral response into three types: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave. Short-wave infrared cameras, sensitive to wavelengths between 0.9 to 2.5 micrometers, are often used in industrial applications for detecting hot spots in equipment. Mid-wave infrared cameras, operating in the 3 to 5 micrometer range, excel in surveillance and security due to their sensitivity to ambient temperature changes. Long-wave infrared cameras, functioning between 8 to 14 micrometers, are ideal for military, firefighting, and medical applications, as they detect heat emitted by objects.</p></p>
