<p><strong>The Variable Delivery Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Pump</li><li>Ball Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The variable delivery pump market is primarily divided into two types: piston pumps and ball pumps. Piston pumps utilize a reciprocating piston to create pressure and control fluid flow, making them suitable for high-pressure applications and precise flow rates. In contrast, ball pumps use a ball mechanism to regulate fluid movement, offering simplicity and reliability for transferring liquids. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture, driven by the need for efficient and adaptable fluid handling solutions.</p></p>
