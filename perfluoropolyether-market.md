<p><p>The Perfluoropolyether (PFPE) market is primarily classified into two types: PFPE oil and PFPE grease. PFPE oil is a versatile lubricant known for its stability across a wide temperature range and resistance to oxidation, making it ideal for high-performance applications in aerospace and electronics. PFPE grease, on the other hand, combines PFPE oil with thickening agents, offering excellent lubrication and protection for gears, bearings, and other industrial components under extreme conditions. Both types are essential for enhancing equipment reliability and longevity.</p></p>
