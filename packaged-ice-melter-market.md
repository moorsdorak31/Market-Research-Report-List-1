<p><ul><li>Products before Snow</li><li>Products after Snow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaged ice melter market consists of two main types: products designed for use before snow accumulation, and those for application after snow has fallen. Pre-snow products are typically preventative, aimed at reducing ice bonding and making snow removal easier when it does occur. These often contain materials that inhibit ice formation. In contrast, post-snow products are formulated to quickly melt existing ice and snow, facilitating safer mobility on surfaces like driveways and sidewalks after precipitation.</p></p>
