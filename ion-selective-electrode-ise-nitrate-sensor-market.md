<p><p>The Ion-selective Electrode (ISE) nitrate sensor market primarily comprises two key types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane sensors. Liquid membrane sensors utilize a specially formulated liquid phase that selectively interacts with nitrate ions, providing high sensitivity and rapid response times. In contrast, PVC membrane sensors incorporate a solid PVC matrix that facilitates ion exchange, offering stability and durability. Both types serve critical applications in environmental monitoring, agriculture, and water quality testing, enhancing the detection and analysis of nitrate levels efficiently.</p></p>
