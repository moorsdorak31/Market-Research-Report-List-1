<p><p>The automotive parking system market comprises three main types: Mechanical Systems, Semi-Automated Systems, and Automated Systems. Mechanical Systems utilize physical components like lift and ramps for efficient space utilization, often requiring minimal operator intervention. Semi-Automated Systems involve some human interaction for parking and retrieval, combining technology with manual input for improved convenience. In contrast, Automated Systems operate entirely autonomously, using sensors and robotics to manage vehicle parking without human involvement, optimizing space and enhancing overall user experience.</p></p>
