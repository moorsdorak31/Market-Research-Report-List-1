<p><p>Electrodeionization Units (EDI) utilize an electrochemical process to purify water, combining ion exchange membranes and electric current for efficient ion removal. The market is distinguished by two membrane types: homogeneous and heterogeneous. Homogeneous membranes have a uniform structure, promoting consistent ion exchange and flow rates, while heterogeneous membranes feature varying compositions, enhancing selectivity and ion transport. Both types cater to different applications, influencing their performance, cost-effectiveness, and suitability in water treatment and industrial processes.</p></p>
