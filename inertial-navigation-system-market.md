<p><ul><li>Mechanical Gyro</li><li>Ring Laser Gyro</li><li>Fiber Optics Gyro</li><li>MEMS</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inertial navigation system (INS) market includes various types of gyroscopes, each serving different applications. Mechanical gyros utilize spinning masses for orientation, while ring laser gyros use laser technology for precise measurements. Fiber optic gyros leverage light transmission through optical fibers for accurate navigation. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) gyros are compact and cost-effective, suited for consumer electronics and small vehicles. Other types encompass hybrid systems and advanced sensors, catering to diverse industries like aerospace, automotive, and robotics.</p></p>
