<p><p>The plastic rapid prototyping machine market encompasses three primary types: Rapid Prototyping, 3D Printing, and Rapid Casting. Rapid Prototyping involves creating physical models from digital designs quickly, often using various additive manufacturing techniques. 3D Printing, a subset of rapid prototyping, specifically utilizes layered material deposition to fabricate intricate shapes directly from digital files. Rapid Casting focuses on producing quick molds, allowing for faster metal part production. Together, these technologies facilitate efficient product development in diverse industries.</p></p>
