<p><p>The 3D hip and knee implant market can be categorized into three main types based on material: metal, ceramic, and polyethylene. Metal implants, typically made from titanium or cobalt-chromium, offer strength and durability. Ceramic implants provide a smooth, wear-resistant surface, reducing friction and potential wear particles. Polyethylene implants, commonly used as the bearing surface, are lightweight and have good shock absorption properties. Each type has distinct advantages, impacting choices based on patient needs, longevity, and biocompatibility.</p></p>
