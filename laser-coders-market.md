<p><p>The laser coders market encompasses various technologies including Fibre Laser Coders, CO2 Laser Coders, and Vanadate Laser Coders. Fibre Laser Coders utilize a solid-state laser, offering high efficiency and versatility for marking materials like metal and plastics. CO2 Laser Coders employ a gas laser suitable for marking organic materials such as wood and paper. Vanadate Laser Coders are less common but provide a solid-state solution, known for precision in industrial applications. Each type serves distinct marking needs across different industries.</p></p>
