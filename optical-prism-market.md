<p><p>The optical prism market includes several types, each serving distinct functions. Right angle prisms are widely used for image inversion and reflection. Diamond prisms are known for their minimal optical aberrations. Pentagonal prisms offer unique beam deviation capabilities. TIR (Total Internal Reflection) prisms are essential for laser applications, while cube beamsplitters enable light division in optical systems. Roof prisms alter the orientation of images, and the "other" category includes specialized prisms for niche applications, expanding versatility in optical technologies.</p></p>
