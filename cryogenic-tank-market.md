<p><p>The cryogenic tank market is divided into two primary types: stationary cryogenic tanks and trailer-type cryogenic tanks. Stationary cryogenic tanks are fixed installations designed for the bulk storage of gases at low temperatures, commonly used in industries like healthcare, aerospace, and energy. In contrast, trailer-type cryogenic tanks are mobile units that allow for the transportation of cryogenic liquids, offering flexibility for temporary storage and distribution in various applications. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient cryogenic solutions.</p></p>
