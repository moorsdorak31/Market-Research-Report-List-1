<p><p>The bottle sealing wax market primarily comprises two types: beads and bricks. Bottle sealing wax beads are small, round pieces that melt easily and are ideal for quick, efficient sealing of bottles. They offer convenience and precision for various applications. In contrast, bottle sealing wax bricks are larger, solid blocks that require more preparation time for melting but allow for larger batches and customization. Both types serve essential roles in preserving contents and enhancing aesthetic appeal in packaging.</p></p>
