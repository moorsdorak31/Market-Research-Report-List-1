<p><p>The desktop air purifier market encompasses three main types: passive, active, and hybrid. Passive purifiers rely on natural airflow and filters to remove pollutants, such as HEPA filters capturing particulate matter. Active purifiers utilize mechanisms like ionization or UV light to actively eliminate contaminants from the air. Hybrid models combine both technologies, offering enhanced purification through filtration and active methods, providing versatile options for consumers seeking efficient air cleaning solutions for their indoor environments.</p></p>
