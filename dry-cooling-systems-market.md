<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dry Cooling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cooled Condensers (ACCs)</li><li>Air Cooled Heat Exchangers (ACHEs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry cooling systems market comprises primarily two types: Air Cooled Condensers (ACCs) and Air Cooled Heat Exchangers (ACHEs). ACCs use ambient air to condense refrigerants in refrigeration processes, ideal for power plants and industrial applications, maintaining efficiency in water-scarce areas. ACHEs, on the other hand, transfer heat between fluids without water, effectively utilized in chemical processing and HVAC systems. Both technologies enhance energy efficiency while minimizing water usage, supporting sustainable practices in various industries.</p></p>
