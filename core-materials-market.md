<p><p>Core materials in manufacturing typically include foam, honeycomb, and balsa, each offering distinct properties and applications. Foam cores are lightweight, provide excellent thermal insulation, and are used in various composite structures. Honeycomb cores, featuring a hexagonal structure, deliver high strength-to-weight ratios and are ideal for aerospace and automotive industries. Balsa wood, known for its high strength and low density, is commonly used in marine and construction applications, enhancing performance and sustainability in composite manufacturing.</p></p>
