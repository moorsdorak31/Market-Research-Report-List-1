<p><p>The sheet metal equipment market encompasses various machine types essential for metal fabrication. Power presses are used for stamping and shaping metal sheets. Arbor presses provide leverage for small-scale pressing tasks. Swaging machines are designed for shaping and forming metal parts by radially compressing them. Bending machines are employed to create bends and angles in sheet metal, while hydraulic shearing machines cut through metal sheets with precision using hydraulic force. Each equipment type caters to specific metalworking needs and processes.</p></p>
