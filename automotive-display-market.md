<p><p>The automotive display market features various types, primarily including TFT-LCD and PMLCD displays. TFT-LCD (Thin Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Display) is renowned for its vibrant colors and sharp images, commonly used in dashboards and infotainment systems. PMLCD (Passive Matrix Liquid Crystal Display) offers a simpler design with lower cost and power consumption, mainly utilized in basic applications. Additionally, other display technologies such as OLED, AMOLED, and mini-LED are emerging, enhancing visual quality and energy efficiency in vehicles.</p></p>
