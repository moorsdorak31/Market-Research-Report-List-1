<p><p>The Metal Detector for Rubber market features two primary types: Conveyor Type and Tunnel Type Metal Detectors. Conveyor Type Metal Detectors integrate with production lines, scanning materials on moving belts to detect metallic contaminants in rubber products. In contrast, Tunnel Type Metal Detectors envelop materials as they pass through, offering a broader detection area for various rubber forms. Both types enhance product safety and quality, ensuring compliance with industry standards by preventing metallic impurities from reaching consumers.</p></p>
