<p><ul><li>Suction Cup</li><li>Friction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper feeder machine market comprises two primary types: suction cup and friction feeders. Suction cup feeders use vacuum mechanisms to pick and transport sheets, ensuring precise handling of delicate or thin materials. In contrast, friction feeders rely on the friction between rollers to move stacks of paper, making them ideal for thicker substrates. Each type serves specific applications in industries like printing, packaging, and manufacturing, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in various workflows.</p></p>
