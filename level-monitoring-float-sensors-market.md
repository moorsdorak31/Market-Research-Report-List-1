<p><p>Level monitoring float sensors are essential for measuring liquid levels in various applications. The top-mounted type is installed above the liquid surface, using a float that moves vertically with the liquid's level, making it ideal for tanks with limited space. Conversely, side-mounted sensors are fixed on the tank's side, offering a robust solution for larger tanks. Both types differ in installation requirements and operational contexts, catering to diverse industrial needs while ensuring accurate level monitoring and management.</p></p>
