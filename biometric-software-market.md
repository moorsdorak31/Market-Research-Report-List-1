<p><p>The biometric software market is segmented into three key types of authentication. Single Factor Authentication relies on one biometric trait, such as a fingerprint, for user verification. Two Factor Authentication combines a biometric identifier with another factor, like a PIN, enhancing security. Multi Factor Authentication offers the highest level of protection by utilizing multiple biometric traits alongside additional verification methods. This layered approach helps organizations protect sensitive data, providing robust security solutions tailored to diverse user needs and risk levels.</p></p>
