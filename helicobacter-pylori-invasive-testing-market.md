<p><ul><li>Histology</li><li>Rapid Urease Test (RUT)</li><li>HP Culture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Helicobacter pylori invasive testing market comprises three main types: histology, rapid urease test (RUT), and HP culture. Histology involves microscopic examination of gastric tissue samples to identify H. pylori presence. The RUT is a quick assay that detects urease, an enzyme produced by H. pylori, in biopsy specimens. HP culture involves growing H. pylori from tissue samples in a laboratory setting to confirm infection and assess antibiotic susceptibility. These methods are critical for accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment of gastric conditions.</p></p>
