<p><p>The low-flush toilets market primarily consists of two types: single-flush and dual-flush toilets. Single-flush toilets utilize a single mechanism for flushing, typically using 1.6 gallons per flush or less, providing a uniform flush for all wastes. In contrast, dual-flush toilets offer two flushing options—one for liquid waste and another for solid waste—allowing for water savings by using approximately 0.8 gallons for liquid flushes and up to 1.6 gallons for solids. This differentiation caters to varying consumer preferences and environmental concerns.</p></p>
