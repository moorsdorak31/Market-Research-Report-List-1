<p><strong>The Automotive Side Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class A Laminated Glass</li><li>Class B Laminated Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive side glass market includes three primary types: Class A laminated glass, Class B laminated glass, and tempered glass. Class A laminated glass offers enhanced safety and noise reduction by combining two glass layers with an interlayer, commonly found in windshields. Class B laminated glass is used for side and rear windows, providing similar benefits with different thickness specifications. Tempered glass, known for its strength and thermal resistance, is widely utilized for side windows, shatter-proofing and ensuring passenger safety.</p></p>
