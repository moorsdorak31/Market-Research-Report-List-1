<p><p>The Non-Isolated DC-DC Converter market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I converters, such as buck and boost converters, efficiently step down or step up voltage levels without electrical isolation, making them suitable for many applications requiring compact design and high efficiency. Type II converters, including buck-boost converters, allow for more versatility by enabling output voltage regulation above and below the input voltage. Both types are crucial for power management in various electronic devices.</p></p>
