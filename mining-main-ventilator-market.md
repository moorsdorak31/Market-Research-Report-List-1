<p><strong>The Mining Main Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Ventilator</li><li>Centrifugal Ventilator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mining main ventilator market primarily consists of two types: axial ventilators and centrifugal ventilators. Axial ventilators operate by moving air in a straight line, making them suitable for applications that require high airflow rates with lower pressure. In contrast, centrifugal ventilators utilize a rotating impeller to increase air pressure and are ideal for overcoming resistance in long or complex duct systems. Both types are essential for maintaining safe and efficient air circulation in mining operations.</p></p>
