<p><strong>The Flow Soldering Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Flow Soldering Machine</li><li>Vapour Phase Flow Soldering Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flow Soldering Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Convection Flow Soldering Machines and Vapour Phase Flow Soldering Machines. Convection Flow Soldering Machines utilize hot air to evenly distribute heat across the circuit boards, facilitating the soldering process through convection. In contrast, Vapour Phase Flow Soldering Machines employ a vaporized liquid to achieve precise temperature control, ensuring uniform soldering without the risk of thermal damage. Both technologies cater to different industrial needs in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
